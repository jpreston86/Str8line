H2Ocean Recommended Aftercare Instructions

Piercing Aftercare Spray

Spray directly on piercing and allow to air dry. Apply as needed to the affected areas. Recommended use is 3 to 6 times per day. H2Ocean’s piercing aftercare spray can also be used as a sea salt soak when needed for infections, keloids and other irritations.

Aquatat Tattoo Ointment

Wash your tattoo thoroughly with a mild antimicrobial soap and warm water. Apply a very thin layer of Aquatat™ ointment making sure to rub into the skin completely. Reapply Aquatat™ as needed during the day. After day 3 follow directions of Ultimate Tattoo Care at H2Ocean.

Ocean Care Skin Moisturizing Cream

Wash your tattoo thoroughly with a mild antimicrobial soap and warm water. Squeeze a small amount of Ocean Care™ on your fingertips. Gently apply a thin layer of Ocean Care™ on your tattoo until it is completely absorbed. Reapply Ocean Care™ as needed during the day. After day 3 follow directions of Ultimate Tattoo Care at H2Ocean.
